22 /**
23 * @brief Represents a physical Hue device (e.g. a bulb, a dimmer switch, a motion sensor)
24 *
25 * A device groups together the control/state services it exposes. In hue4cpp those
26 * services are surfaced as the Light and Sensor objects the device owns, alongside the
27 * product metadata reported by the bridge (model, archetype, software version, ...).
28 *
29 * The contained Light and Sensor objects are the same reactive objects returned by
30 * Bridge::getLights() / Bridge::getSensors(); subscribing to their properties gives
31 * real-time updates. The lists are always ordered to follow the device's declared

34 * @note Device, like Light and Sensor, is an ObservableObject and is therefore
